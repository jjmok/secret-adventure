My Secret Adventure

On a perfectly normal, perfectly pretty day, a journey to find a lost toy becomes a magical adventure of meeting new friends and collecting treasures. Here is a beautiful story that is personalised to its owner’s name, told with playful words, peppered with local elements, and brought to life with beautiful illustrations.

How it Works

Step 1

My Secret Adventure

For the child, the delight begins when they see the magical book cover. Wow, a story about my secret adventure? Wah! The excitement builds.

Step 2

Each name spells a different adventure

The letters of every child’s unique name will determine how their adventure is shaped - will they meet some squabbling elephants in the woods, an old lady selling kuih in a clearing or apple-pie making kangaroo? No two stories are the same, and only the child’s name will determine their special journey.

Step 3

Make the child a part of the story

Choose from boy or girl - perfectly customising the book for its intended recipient..

Step 4

Make it special

Say something to mark an important milestone, or simply let them know how much you want them to sayang this customised adventure.
