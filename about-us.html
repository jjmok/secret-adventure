About us

Our story began when a group of old friends and new friends banded together to embark on a secret adventure.

Our adventure is constantly evolving and expanding, inspired by our children, our cultures, the country which we live in (Malaysia), and those that we travel to.

The end goal? To make sure that each and every book we create is not only beautiful to look at, but inspires a child (and adult!) to explore, experience, learn new things, and most of all, open the mind to (magical!) possibilities.
